Account & Billing

üí≥ Account & Billing

Answers to common account and payment questions.

How do I change my password? +

Go to your account settings by clicking your profile icon in the top right corner, then select "Security". Click "Change Password", enter your current password, then your new password twice, and confirm. You'll receive a confirmation email once the change is saved.

How do I update my payment details? +

Navigate to your account settings and select "Billing". From there you can add, remove, or update your saved payment methods. Changes take effect immediately for future bookings.

When are payouts sent to tutors? +

Tutors receive monthly payouts by the 5th of each month for all sessions completed in the previous calendar month. You can view a full breakdown of your earnings in your tutor dashboard.

How do I cancel my subscription? +

You can cancel your subscription at any time from account settings under "Billing & Subscription". Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period. No refunds are issued for partial billing periods.

How do I delete my account? +

To permanently delete your account, go to account settings and select "Delete Account" at the bottom of the page. Alternatively, email privacy@tutorslink.com with your request. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Solutions to common technical questions and issues.

What browsers are supported? +

TutorsLink works best on the latest versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. We recommend keeping your browser up to date for the best experience. Internet Explorer is not supported.

Is there a mobile app? +

We do not currently have a dedicated mobile app, but TutorsLink is fully responsive and works great in mobile browsers on both iOS and Android. A dedicated app is on our roadmap ‚Äî stay tuned!

What video tool do you use for sessions? +

Sessions can be conducted using the TutorsLink platform's built-in video tool, or by agreement between student and tutor using a preferred tool such as Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams. Confirm your preferred method with your tutor before your first session.

I'm having connection issues ‚Äî what should I do? +

First, try refreshing the page and checking your internet connection. If the issue persists, try a different browser or clear your cache and cookies. If you experience connection problems during a live session, let your tutor know immediately via chat and try reconnecting. If the session cannot proceed, contact support@tutorslink.com and we'll make it right.
